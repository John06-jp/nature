Welcome to Majestic Falls

Majestic Falls National Park is designed to make the beauty of nature accessible to everyone. From accessible walking trails to tactile exhibits, we ensure that all visitors can experience the wonder of the falls safely and comfortably.

Accessible Features

Wheelchair-Friendly Trails

Our main paths are paved and slope gently, allowing smooth access for wheelchair users and visitors with mobility aids.

Braille and Audio Guides

Information boards are available in Braille, and visitors can borrow audio devices with narration in multiple languages.

Quiet Zones

Designated calm spaces help visitors with sensory sensitivities relax and enjoy their visit at their own pace.
